Frequently Asked Questions

A set of frequently asked questions (FAQ) that may come to mind for users when using the “Rafeeq” application and their answers:

How can I use the Rafiq app?

To use the Rafiq app, download it from the app store, then open the app and follow the registration or login steps.

Can I explore places near me?

Yes, Rafiq app uses geolocation technology to display places and activities available near you. Once you enable location, the app will display various options near you to make the selection process easier.

Can I customize suggestions based on my interests?

Yes, Rafiq provides personalized suggestions based on your personal interests and budget. Once you specify your interests such as type of activity or preferred destination, the app will provide the best options that suit you.

Can I set my budget?

Of course! The Rafiq app allows you to set your budget before exploring places. Based on that, you will be able to easily find activities and places that fit your budget.

How do I book a venue or activity?

After choosing the place or activity you like, you can simply click on the “Book Now” or “Book” button to go to the booking page. Follow the required steps, such as selecting the date and time of the reservation, then complete the payment process if necessary.

Can I modify the reservation after it is confirmed?

It depends on the type of activity or venue. Some activities and hotels allow you to modify your reservation, while others may require you to cancel your reservation and make a new one. You can always contact customer service for assistance.

Can I see other users' reviews?

Yes, you can view other users' reviews and opinions about places and activities within the app. These reviews will help you make an informed decision before booking.

Does the app offer special offers or discounts?

Yes! Rafeeq app offers exclusive offers and discounts to users from time to time. Make sure to check the offers section within the app to avail the latest discounts.

Contact us

We are always here to listen to you and help you with any questions or issues you may have. Whether you need technical assistance, a booking inquiry, or would like to provide feedback, the Rafeeq team is always ready to support you.
